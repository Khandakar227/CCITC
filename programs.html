Member Recruitment Programme

Chittagong College Information Technology Club is a platform that will provide students with opportunities to discuss various IT issues outside the classroom and enhance the skills of students other than regular curriculum. Current activities include: 1. Programming and Problem Solving 2. IoT and Robotics 3. Web Development and Graphic Design 4.
