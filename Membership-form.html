Member Recruitment

Chittagong College Information Technology Club is a platform that will provide students with opportunities to discuss various IT issues outside the classroom and enhance the skills of students other than regular curriculum. Current activities include:
1. Programming and Problem Solving
2. IoT and Robotics
3. Web Development and Graphic Design
4. Data Science and Machine Learning
There will be one class per week for any of the above four topics. To attend the classes being a member is mandatory. So here we announce "Member Recruitment Programme 1.0".If you want to be a member, you must fill out an application form. Membership forms are available from today. Contact your section agent for the membership form. A certain number of applicants will be selected as members. Every selected member will get a confirmation message soon (via SMS/mail).
